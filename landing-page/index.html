Frequently Asked Questions

What is the ClariTask Chrome extension?

ClariTask is a smart AI-powered task manager that replaces your Chrome new tab page. It helps you stay focused and productive by combining task management, a focus timer, and AI assistance.

Is the ClariTask extension free?

Yes! The extension is completely free to install. Basic task management and the focus timer do not cost anything.

How does the AI task breakdown work?

When you have a large or ambiguous task, the built-in AI assistant breaks it down into actionable subtasks with time estimates. You can add these directly to your list to turn complex projects into simple steps.

Is my data secure and private?

Yes! ClariTask uses Chrome's built-in local and sync storage API. Your tasks reside locally on your browser.
